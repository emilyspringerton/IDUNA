VS8 Specification: Auraborialis Reboot Protocol
VS8, known as the Auraborialis Reboot Protocol, is the continuity reset and mythic cycle layer of the IDUNA ecosystem
. It provides the canonical mechanism for world resets without invalidating prior reality, ensuring that in an immortal event-sourced universe, the past is never erased but rather recontextualized
.
1. Mission and Core Philosophy
The core idea of Auraborialis is that reality is cyclical, but memory is absolute
. It allows the world to begin again by emerging a new continuity branch from the existing archive
.
Archive Invariance: The Event Store is never deleted or rewritten; historical truth is preserved
.
Projection Reset: While the archive is permanent, the "True Store" (world state projections) can be reinitialized for a new epoch
.
2. Definitional Properties
Auraborialis is a state reinitialization boundary that obeys the following rules:
Cycle Identity: Every reboot receives a permanent, unambiguous identifier (e.g., cycle_id, epoch_id, or continuity_id)
.
Identity Persistence: Human identities transcend cycles. Accounts, gamertags, and authority structures persist unless explicitly rebased
.
Archived Continuities: Prior worlds are not "wiped" but become inspectable historical continuities
.
3. System-Level Reset Scope
What is Reset:
World State: MMO positional reality and spatial constructs
.
Economic Systems: Virtual economies and currency distributions
.
Social Metrics: Tournament standings, market seasons, and social rankings
.
What is NOT Reset:
User Accounts: Identity remains continuous
.
Legal Entitlements: Licenses (e.g., multibox licenses) and subscriptions
.
Governance Roles: Agent and authority roles persist
.
Audit History: The full event log remains intact
.
4. Technical Implementation Mechanism
Auraborialis is implemented through non-destructive re-projection
.
Namespace Rebinding: The system closes the active projection scope and starts a new projection namespace
.
Query Redirection: "Current world" queries are rebound to the new namespace while cross-cycle queries remain available for historical records
.
5. Event Store Vocabulary
The transition between reality cycles is defined by specific system-level events:
AuraborialisDeclared: The formal intent to reset.
ContinuityCycleClosed: The conclusion of the current epoch.
ProjectionResetExecuted: The technical clearing of projection state.
ContinuityCycleStarted: The initialization of the new world instance.
LegacyCycleArchived: The formal movement of state into the historical records
.
6. Governance and Authority
The declaration of Auraborialis is a constitutional event
. It cannot be triggered arbitrarily and requires:
A specific Governance Authority role.
Multi-agent confirmation (depending on the specific world charter).
The emission of system-level events to ensure historical legitimacy
.
7. VS8 "Done" Definition
A successful implementation of the Auraborialis protocol is verified when:
The cycle identity model is established and first-class.
Projection namespaces reset cleanly without data loss in the Event Store.
Identity persists seamlessly across the cycle boundary.
Prior cycles remain fully queryable as archives or "halls of history"
.
Governance controls for reboot authority are operational
.
